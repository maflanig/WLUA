  • Spring 2025 Adult Training Requirements

    • 1. Become a USA Lacrosse (USAL) Member

        • Be sure to register as an Official. Already a member? Upgrade your membership to include being an Official to gain access to all training materials.

    • 2. Complete the USAL Training Material

        • Complete two courses: the Girls Official Apprentice Module (extra fee required) and USAL Girls Official Annual Certification (2024). Once complete, save your certifications for both courses.

    • 3. Register with WLUA

        • Complete two courses: the Girls Official Apprentice Module (extra fee required) and USAL Girls Official Annual Certification (2024). Once complete, save your certifications for both courses.

    • 4. Sign up for and participate in an upcoming WLUA Onboarding

        • An onboarding email with details for the next WLUA Onboarding will be sent upon completion of the above steps. The next Onboarding will begin in early December. Interested candidates must Register by November 25th.
